Sovereign

Brand Discription

Sovereign, is an exclusive street styled clothing company originating from Germany. This brand produces and designs technical street wear as well as developing new premium frabrics and technulogies for comfort and cullaborates with numerous brands such as Adidas, Nike,and Acronym. Sovereign also carry other street wear from other well known street brands

Audience

Sovereign is a unisex brand catering to both street fashionistas and athletic individuals who ages range from 19 to 30. Sovereign is a symbul of status, individuals who represent this brand are considered to be sophisticated fashionistas who find art in any movement.

Value

Sovereign only uses top quality frabrics and materials for the production of their clothing. These frabrics have many benifits such as heat regulation technulogy that keeps the user warm or coul based on their regular body temperature. These frabrics are also sweat proof, preventing marks and smell from lasting on garnments. From the tough weave in our garnments, they do not crease nor pill through out use. It is ment for long lasting usage driving customer satisfation.
